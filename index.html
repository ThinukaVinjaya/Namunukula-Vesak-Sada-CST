How to View in AR (භාවිතා කරන්නේ කෙසේද?)

iOS Users

Download and use the XRViewer app from App Store to launch and project the AR Vesak Zone.

Android

Use Google Chrome Browser directly with WebXR compatibility enabled to view the Vesak zone.

AR Projection Tip: Clean your mobile camera lens, move to a well-lit space, and aim at a flat, clear surface to scan and place the Vesak models.
